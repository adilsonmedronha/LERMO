How to Play

  • Guess the word in 6 tries.
  • Tile colors change based on how close your guess is to the word.
  • Spell the letters using LIBRAS.
  • Words may contain repeated letters.
  • The system detects signs through your camera.

In addition to the LIBRAS signs, consider the following control signs:

  • sends a guess (word).
  • deletes a letter.

Below the camera, you can find a table with the signs.
